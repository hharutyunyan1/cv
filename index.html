My Bio is:

Born as an Armenian native in Gyumri, Armenia and raised in Los Angeles, California, Hakob Harutyunyan was brought up in a scientific/creative household. Since the early 2000s, he showed promise in mathematics, chess, and in the hard sciences. This all led to the completion of a Bachelor of Science in Mathematics from the California State University of Los Angeles in 2015. Full of excitement and innovative drive, Hakob went right into the graduate program in Math at Cal State LA and received his Master of Science in Applied Mathematics in 2018. As a young Armenian full of excitement and intriguing ideas about the advancement of our youth, he stands in the forefront of innovative solutions to the bigger problems of the world of mathematics.
